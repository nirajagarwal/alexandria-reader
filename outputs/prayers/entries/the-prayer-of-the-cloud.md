# The Prayer of The Cloud

This prayer comes from *The Cloud of Unknowing*, an anonymous work of Christian mysticism written in Middle English in the late fourteenth century. The author, writing for a young contemplative, teaches a way of prayer that moves beyond thoughts and images toward God in darkness. Between the soul and God, the author writes, lies a cloud of unknowing — and the prayer that pierces it is not elaborate but simple, a single word or phrase lifted up repeatedly. This prayer, which the author calls "the little word," is offered as the heart of that practice. It is prayed silently, steadily, until words give way to presence.
